Welcome to Melbourne International Policy Institute.

Since founded in 2020, Melbourne International Policy Institute is committed to helping people make meaning out of the impact geopolitics has on their lives by empowering them to get involved in the issues that matter to them most. We inhabit an unusual space that includes the expertise of a think tank, the savvy understanding of social media, the relationships of teachers and thought leaders, and the inspiration of a civic activist.

OUR PROGRAMS ,CAREERS & INTERNSHIPS

THE INTERNATIONAL ECONOMIC PROGRAM

The International Economics program aims to explain developments in the international economy, and influence policy. It does so by undertaking independent analytical research.The International Economics program contributes to the MIPI’s core publications: policy briefs and policy analyses.

Learn More

THE INTERNATIONAL SECURITY PROGRAM

The importance of a rule-based global order to support its defense and security interests is well emphasized. MIPI’s international program explores some of the challenges to maintaining international peace and security, both as they relate to Australia’s direct engagement as well as contributing to broader international research efforts on multilateral security issues.

THE POWER & DIPLOMACY PROGRAM

The Power and Diplomacy Program researches on the implications of the world's economic transformation for war, peace and the global balance of power in the twenty-first century. It does this using innovative and data driven methodologies to understand shifts in political economies, military balance and diplomatic networks. The program is organized around two major annual projects – the Asia Power Index and the Global Diplomacy Index – and holds a series of events on and in the region, independently and in partnership with other organizations.

THE CLIMATE AND SECURITY PROGRAM

Our Climate and Security Policy Program builds awareness of the transformational impacts climate change will have on security in the Indo-Pacific and develops policy responses to reduce the perilous consequences. The Centre’s impact stems from the credibility and significance of the original research it produces, its practical contributions to the public debate, and its effective engagement with institutional partners in Australia and abroad.

SUPPORT US
