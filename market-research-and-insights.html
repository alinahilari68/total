CAREERS --Director of Strategic Communications

Reporting directly to the Executive Director, this is a key leadership role with influence across the organization. The Director of Strategic Communications will work across MIPI to develop and shape the organization’s communications strategy. This will involve overseeing the MIPI Strategist, the MIPI Podcast, social media, promotion of MIPI products, speech writing and media engagement. The Director of Strategic Communications will work with all forms of media, including press releases and digital campaigns, print materials, social media, audio, and video. As a team, you will develop MIPI’s communications strategy and work across MIPI to deliver our strategic priorities, including influencing key policy and decision makers, reaching broad and diverse audiences, and attracting new partners and supporters. The closing date for applications is Friday, 7 Oct 2022 – an early application is advised as we reserve the right to close the vacancy early if suitable applications are received.
